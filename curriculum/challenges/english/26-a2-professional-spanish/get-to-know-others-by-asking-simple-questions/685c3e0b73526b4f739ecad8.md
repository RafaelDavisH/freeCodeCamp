## Greetings

- `¿Cómo estás?` - How are you?

## Emotions and Conditions

- `Feliz` - Happy.
- `Cansada` - Tired (feminine).

- `Escuchar música` - To listen to music.

- `Me gusta` - I like.
- `Buena energía` - Good energy.

- `Un poco` - A little.
- `Gracias` - Thank you.
- `Sí` - Yes.
- `¿Y tú?` - And you?
